Metrics
Big Mac Index

The Big Mac Index is published by The Economist as an informal way of measuring the purchasing power parity (PPP) between two currencies and provides a test of the extent to which market exchange rates result in goods costing the same in different countries. [Wikipedia]

Consumer price index

A consumer price index (CPI) measures changes in the price level of market basket of consumer goods and services purchased by households. [Wikipedia]
